      Last Updated: March 13, 2025

      On April 1, 2025, Wisconsin will hold a nonpartisan election for a 10-year term on the Wisconsin Supreme Court. The candidates are competing to fill the seat of incumbent Justice Ann Walsh Bradley who is retiring. The primary election was canceled given that only two candidates are running. The candidates are Dane County Circuit Court judge Susan Crawford, who has been endorsed by the Wisconsin Democratic Party, and Waukesha County Circuit Court judge Brad Schimel, who previously served as attorney general, a position he ran for as a Republican.
